# Stylin by Jojo - Luxury Personal Styling Website

A beautiful, responsive website for a luxury personal styling business in South Florida.

## 📋 Features

- **Responsive Design** - Works seamlessly on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices
- **Modern Aesthetics** - Elegant luxury branding with gold and dark color scheme
- **Multiple Pages**:
  - Homepage with hero section, services overview, and testimonials
  - About page with biography and credentials
  - Services page with detailed service descriptions and pricing packages
  - Process page explaining the styling methodology
  - Contact page with contact form and FAQ

- **Smooth Navigation** - Mobile-friendly hamburger menu
- **Professional Styling** - Playfair Display and Montserrat fonts for sophisticated look
- **Interactive Elements** - Hover effects and smooth transitions

## 📝 Customization

### Update Business Information
- Email: Update `hello@stylinbyjojo.com` in contact.html
- Phone: Update `(561) 333-4444` in contact.html
- Social Links: Update social media URLs in footer sections

### Add Your Images
Replace placeholder images with your own:
- Hero image in index.html
- Service images in services.html
- Use high-quality images for best results

### Modify Services & Pricing
- Edit service descriptions in services.html
- Update pricing in the packages section
- Customize service inclusions lists
